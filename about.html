About Municipal Corporation

The Municipal Corporation is a local government body responsible for providing essential civic services to the citizens of the city. It works to maintain public infrastructure, sanitation, water supply, road maintenance, and other important services to ensure a healthy and safe environment for residents.

Our Services

  • Waste management and sanitation
  • Water supply management
  • Street lighting maintenance
  • Road repair and infrastructure maintenance
  • Public health and hygiene services
  • Citizen grievance redressal system

Our Mission

To provide efficient and transparent civic services while addressing citizen complaints quickly and effectively through digital platforms like the Municipal Complaint Management System.

Our Vision

To build a clean, smart, and sustainable city by improving urban infrastructure and ensuring active citizen participation in municipal governance.
